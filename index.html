Penida Beach

Discover the pristine beauty of Penida Beach in Nusa Penida. Ideal for snorkeling and diving, it boasts crystal-clear waters and dramatic cliffs.

Borobudur Temple

The world's largest Buddhist temple in Central Java. This UNESCO site features intricate rock carvings and offers stunning views of the sunrise.

Mount Bromo

Experience the majestic Mount Bromo in East Java, renowned for its stunning sunrise views and unique landscape in Bromo Tengger Semeru National Park.
